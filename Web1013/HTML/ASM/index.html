What's an NFT ?

NFTs are tokens that we can use to represent ownership of unique items.
They let us tokenise things like art, collectibles, even real estate.

How do NFTs work ?

NFTs give the ability to assign or claim ownership of any unique piece of digital data,
trackable by using Ethereum's blockchain as a public ledger.
